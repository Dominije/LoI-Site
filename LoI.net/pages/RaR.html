Ranks and Roles

People may go up in rank if they are working really hard for the clan. This will be regardless of their bloodlines stats, and regardless of if they meet the above requirements or not. This will be decided by Senate.

If you have come from a different clan with a high rank and you meet the requirements to go into any of the ranks, it will be decided by the Senate on what role you get

The minion count listed below on the ranks is just a guideline it is not set in stone and is case by case it is a basic guideline of where people should be at to achieve those ranks

You will need to meet several of the requirements below to move up in rank not just one or two of them.

PLEASE NOTE AN ACTIVE MINION MEANS A PERSON THAT COMES TO THE SIM GETS INVOLVED AND PARTICIPATES IN THE FAMILY

PROGENY

This is the starting rank of the clan where you learn how to play bloodlines and get to know everyone

Rights and Permissions

  • Clan rezz rights
  • group chats

OPTIO

Once you have your first few hunts and you know how to turn people on your own this will be your rank. Attaining this rank means that you can look after yourself and your minions and maintain your vital levels.

Rights and Permissions

  • Clan rezz rights
  • group chats
  • Invite to Progeny Rank

Consul

To get to this rank you will need to show that you are capable of turning someone on your own. You will need to have at least 2 active minions by this stage. You will need to have 5 achievements. You will need to have 10+ personal souls. You will be able to invite people to the clan as the human tag but will need to go to a Legate/Tribune to get their tag changed. You will be able to send welcome notices for your minions but no other notices. You will also need to be able to show you are capable of taking care of your minions and you are helping them and teaching them also.

Rights and Permissions

  • Clan rezz rights
  • group chats
  • Invite to Progeny and Optio Rank
  • Send Welcome Notices

PRIMUS

You are now a skilled bloodlines player, you know what you are doing and shouldn’t need help in maintaining your minions or issues. You are an active clan member and participate in the clan. You have been in bloodlines for a while

Rights and Permissions

  • Clan rezz rights
  • group chats
  • Invite to Progeny Rank
  • Send Notices

✯LEGATUS/TRIBUNE✯

If you are interested in becoming a member of management, please let your liege or your line head know. You will be put into one or the other not both, senates will be in both of these. If you want to be put forward for management it will no longer be an application process, either your liege or line head will put your name forward to council they will then take a vote, from there you will be contacted by the managers and interviewed and tested related to the role you are going into. Both of these roles require strong bloodlines knowledge, experience in clans and dealing with minions, you will need to have a good reputation and be a respected member of the clan. These roles can be revoked at any stage if we find you to not be suited to them.
