A True Secondlife Bloodlines Family That Rises Above All Others

While we value participation from ALL of our family, there are a few groups that have shown dedication to the Legion at levels above and beyond the standard. These groups are our Senate, Legatus, and Tribunus teams.

Legion Senate

This is the leadership of the clan, everyone in this rank is majorly involved in running the clan, and they make all final decisions. This group of people has been chosen personally by April to run the clan, they are the most experienced and respected role in the clan.

Legion Tribunes - Managed by Verena Brune and Todd Edwyn

This group is responsible for keeping the clan happy, busy and entertained. They help out on the clan land and in the groups. These are the people who help organize events, do achievements, clan meet and greets, games nights, they are social and on hand at the sim, talk in the chats and are on the ground involved in the everyday running of the clan.

The Tribunes do not police the clan they do not ban, eject or make decisions regarding clan members, if you are helping someone or there is an incident they are to refer it to the Legates to deal with.

A Tribune will need to be doing a lot more than just watching chats and hanging on the sim, you are expected to get involved in helping the clan. You are being mentored in how to lead and handle smaller conflicts and situations diplomatically and efficiently

Legion Legatus - Managed by Janeblond and Angelus Males

This group handles conflict resolution, problems with lieges and any major problems within the family. A Legatus will also handle external affairs like issues with other clans, soul trades and issues with bloodlines. The Legatus team members were chosen for this group because we are diplomatic, level headed, unbiased and have demonstrated their ability to handle pressure and stress

As a Legatus, it is your responsibility to make sure conflicts are resolved efficiently and quickly. You proactively diffuse any situation in the chats or on the sims before it gets out of control. You are the authority in the clan.
