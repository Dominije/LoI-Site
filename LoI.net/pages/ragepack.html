Common Concerns and Questions

Giving the pack an area to play does not segregate them from the clan. It affords them an area to play, whether it be RP, parties or events - whatever. The clan is really large and it is hard to keep everyone happy. Just as the clan has a definite "foreign" aspect to it with all our multi-cultural people, we also have a definitive "lycan" part and this should be embraced also.

We do not want any segregation with the pack what so ever, they are part of the family, just as we have different groups for all sorts from the LGBT to Adults, to Role Play, this is exactly the same another group of different players,

The pack is not about ranking it is more about getting involved in something, what you can become in the pack will not reflect in the clan, so please do not mistake this for *power* over anything, the pack is more role play and about running as a group, not run off bloodlines stats and there is a lot of ways to get involved.
