A True Secondlife Bloodlines Family That Rises Above All Others

While the Legion is a place for fun, adventure, and maybe a bit of misbehavior, we do have rules in place to ensure the happiness of the family. Please familiarize yourself with the rules and consequences below, as ignorance is no excuse for breaking them.

General Rules

  • Legion of Immortals Bloodlines Clan is mature NO one under 18 is permitted in the family. (RL age)
    • Membership is also not allowed for avatars newer than 14 days unless voice verified by Senate.
  • Harassment, racism, sexist behavior, gossiping, drama starting, slander and discrimination in any form are not tolerated.
  • No one is permitted to change liege without it going though Senate members permission.
  • Griefing is not allowed on the clan land, the following can be classed as griefing:
    • Using weapons/Spell HUDs
    • Trapping
    • Pushing
    • *This includes Bloodlines War Huds, No one is to kill anyone unless agreed upon or in designated war bases
  • Lagging out the sim
  • Biting without prior consent
  • Begging for money (RL or Linden) in group chat or on clan land
  • Disrespect for Management or other members in group chat
  • Intentional nudity or explicit sex on the clan land outside of the adult area
  • Advertisement for non-clan events or services in main chat group
  • Child Avatars
    • Kids are not allowed in Club unless April is with them
    • Kids at the campfire and on the sim ARE allowed, outside of the adult area.
    • Child avatars will be treated like a child, and must be supervised by an adult at all times if you are incapable of taking care of yourself

Consequences

Violation of the rules above will initiate the following consequences:

  • 1st Offense - Warning
  • 2nd Offense - 3 Day Ban from sim and group
  • 3rd Offense - 2 Week ban and suspension of all group tags
  • 4th Offense - GAME OVER - removed from Legion of Immortals groups completely and removed from clan

These consequences apply to everyone no matter what rank they are**

Drama or feeding into drama on bloodlines sims will NOT be tolerated at all you will get an instant 2 week suspension.

Things to Remember

  • Use the chain of command (Liege-> Legatus -> Senate -> Empress or Caesar) - If you need help ask in the group chat
  • If you rezz something on the clan land please clean up when you are done.
  • Do not hang out in the turning room, or being disruptive during formal turnings.
  • Ranks higher than Consul require Legatus/Senator approval.
  • The Rule of Thumb should be: " Be who you are, be honest at all times and respect your fellow family members as there are real people behind those avis"
  • Dating within the clan
    • if you are lieged to your partner and there is a breakdown in relationship you may reliege with a council members approval
    • REMEMBER - No harassment or public drama will be tolerated.

Any adult or child avi breaking LL TOS will be immediately removed from the clan
